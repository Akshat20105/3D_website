Data Structures and Algorithms

This project involved the implementation of various data structures, including arrays, linked lists, stacks, and queues, ensuring efficient data storage and retrieval. I also worked on algorithms such as sorting and searching algorithms, with a focus on optimizing performance.

MultiVersus

As a mentor, I guided a team in developing a multiplayer game, "MultiVersus," capable of handling up to 50,000 concurrent players. My role included overseeing the game's architecture, managing server-client communication, and ensuring the game's scalability and performance under high load conditions.

Chess AI

In this project, I developed a chess engine using advanced techniques like Monte Carlo Tree Search and alpha-beta pruning. The AI was trained to evaluate board positions and make strategic moves, leading to an intelligent and competitive chess-playing experience.
